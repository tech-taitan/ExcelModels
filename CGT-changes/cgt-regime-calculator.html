Budget Settings

  • Current regime applies a 50% CGT discount to eligible assets held more than 12 months.
  • From 1 July 2027, the Budget proposal replaces the 50% discount with cost base indexation for post-reform gains.
  • The new regime includes a 30% minimum tax on net capital gains, unless the taxpayer type is exempt.
  • Pre-1985 assets keep pre-reform gains outside the modelled CGT base, but post-reform gains are included.
  • New residential property investors are modelled with the lower of discount treatment and indexation/minimum-tax treatment.

Scope

  • Foreign resident CGT rules are not modelled.
  • Company taxpayers, superannuation funds and detailed trust streaming are not modelled.
  • Main residence exemptions and small business CGT concessions are not modelled.
  • Capital losses are applied at portfolio level in both regimes.
  • All amounts are estimates for comparison and planning only.
